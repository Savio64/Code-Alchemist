    Learn to Stay Safe

    Tailored advice for everyone.

    Stranger Danger

    Just like outside, don't talk to strangers online. Never tell them your name or school.

    Free Robux?

    If someone offers free game money or skins, it's a trick! Discuss it with your parents first.

    Secret Passwords

    Your password is like your house key. Only give it to your parents, never to friends online.

    Phishing Emails

    Check the sender's email carefully. Banks won't ask for your password via email.

    Shopping Scams

    If a deal looks too good to be true (80% off brand new iPhones), it probably is.

    Investment Fraud

    Be wary of "guaranteed returns" or pressure to invest in crypto quickly.

    Tech Support Scams

    Microsoft or Apple will NEVER call you to fix a "virus". Hang up immediately.

    Romance Scams

    Be careful if an online friend asks for money for "travel" or "emergencies".

    Grandparent Scam

    If a "grandchild" calls asking for money secretly, call their parents to verify first.
